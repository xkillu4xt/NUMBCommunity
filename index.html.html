Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, join and ask us directly!

Simply click the "Join Now" button at the top of this page or use our invite link: discord.gg/nexuscommunity. After joining, read the rules in #welcome and react to get access to the rest of the server.

Our server is open to all ages, but some channels may contain mature content (clearly marked with NSFW tags). We expect all members to behave maturely regardless of age.

Moderators are chosen from active, helpful community members who demonstrate good judgment. After being a positive member for several months, you may be invited to apply for the team.

Our main rules are: be respectful, no spam, no NSFW content outside marked channels, no self-promotion without permission, and no harassment of any kind. The full rules are posted in #rules.

For general questions, ask in #help. For issues with other members, DM a moderator (names in red/orange). For technical issues with bots, ping @Tech Support.
